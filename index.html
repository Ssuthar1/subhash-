Services

As a web developer, I offer a wide range of services including design, development, e-commerce, responsive design, CMS integration, and maintenance. I collaborate with clients to understand their needs and provide tailored solutions. I stay up-to-date with latest technologies and best practices for modern, secure, and accessible websites.

Web Design

Web design involves creating the look, feel, and functionality of a website to make it appealing, user-friendly, and effective in delivering a message or providing a service. It involves combining art, technology, and usability to create a seamless user experience.

Web Development

Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites, including the coding, programming, and creation of web-based applications. It encompasses a range of skills and technologies to bring a website to life and make it functional for its intended purpose.

Web Maintenance

Website maintenance is the ongoing process of updating, improving, and preserving a website to ensure its optimal performance, security, and functionality. It involves tasks such as fixing bugs, updating content, ensuring security, and ensuring compatibility with new technologies and devices.

CMS

content management system (CMS) is software that allows users to manage and publish digital content without technical expertise. It provides a user-friendly interface for creating, organizing, and publishing website content, and can help streamline the process of maintaining a website.
